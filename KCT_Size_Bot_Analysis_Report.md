### 3.2 Detailed Instructions Example (Chest Measurement)
**Step-by-Step Process:**
1. Stand straight with arms relaxed at your sides
2. Wrap the measuring tape around the fullest part of your chest
3. Keep the tape parallel to the floor
4. Take a deep breath and let it out
5. Record the measurement at normal breathing

**Pro Tips:**
- Keep the measuring tape parallel to the floor
- Don't pull the tape too tight
- Measure over a thin shirt for accuracy
- Have someone help you for best results

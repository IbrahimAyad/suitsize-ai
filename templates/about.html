Our AI Technology

Smart Size AI uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on our extensive customer database to provide accurate size recommendations. Here's how it works:

Data-Driven Learning

Our AI is trained on thousands of real customer measurements and purchase patterns from our menswear business. This gives it unique insights into how different body types and preferences correlate with clothing sizes.

Pattern Recognition

The AI analyzes patterns in customer data to understand relationships between height, weight, age, shopping experience, and clothing sizes. It learns from successful fits to improve recommendations over time.

Confidence Scoring

Each recommendation comes with a confidence score based on the AI's certainty. High confidence means the recommendation is based on many similar customer profiles, while lower confidence suggests you should consider a professional fitting.

Continuous Improvement

Our AI continuously learns from new customer data and feedback, making it more accurate over time. Every interaction helps improve recommendations for future customers.

Why Trust Our AI?

  • Real Data: Trained on actual customer measurements, not generic sizing charts
  • Proven Accuracy: Used successfully by thousands of customers
  • Transparent: Shows confidence levels so you know how reliable each recommendation is
  • Professional Backup: Always recommends professional fitting for best results

Privacy & Security

Your personal information is used only to generate size recommendations and is not stored or shared. We prioritize your privacy and security in everything we do.

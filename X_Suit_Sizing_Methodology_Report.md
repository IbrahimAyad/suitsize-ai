# X Suit Sizing Methodology Report

**Research Date:** December 17, 2025  
**Website:** https://xsuit.com  
**Focus:** Fit Guide, Sizing Methodology, Measurement Process, Data Collection, and Sizing Algorithm

## Executive Summary

X Suit employs a dual approach to sizing that combines an AI-powered predictive sizing tool with traditional measurement-based size charts. Their methodology emphasizes convenience and accuracy while eliminating the need for in-person fittings.

## 1. Two-Method Sizing System

### 1.1 AI Fit Finder (Primary Method)
- **Name:** "Find My Size" / "AI Fit Finder"
- **Process Time:** Less than 1 minute
- **Data Requirements:** Height, Weight, Age (no physical measurements needed)
- **Output:** Recommended size displayed on every product page
- **Target User:** Customers seeking quick, convenient sizing without measuring

### 1.2 Traditional Size Charts (Secondary Method)
- **Purpose:** For customers who prefer manual measurements
- **Method:** Use key body measurements to determine size
- **Format:** Interactive size charts with measurement data
- **Instructions:** Provided through tooltips and visual guides

## 2. Detailed Measurement Methodology

### 2.1 Jacket Measurements
**Available Sizes:** 34-54 (XS to 6XL) in three lengths: Short, Regular, Long

**Length Classification by Height:**
- **Short:** < 5'6"
- **Regular:** 5'6" - 6'1"
- **Long:** > 6'1"

**Measurement Points (all in inches):**
1. **Shoulder Width:** Distance from shoulder seam to shoulder seam across the back
2. **Chest Width:** Across the chest at the fullest point
3. **Waist Width:** At the natural waistline
4. **Hip Width:** At the hips
5. **Center Back Length:** From collar base to hem
6. **Sleeve Length:** From shoulder seam to wrist
7. **Sleeve Bicep Width:** Around the bicep
8. **Sleeve Opening Width:** Around the wrist opening

**Specific Measurement Instruction Example (Shoulder Width):**
- "Lay the jacket face down. Measure from where the shoulder seam meets the sleeve on one side of the jacket straight across to the same point on the other side."

### 2.2 Pants Measurements
**Available Waist Sizes:** 28-46 (XS to 5XL)  
**Available Inseam Lengths:** 28", 30", 32", 34"

**Measurement Points (all in inches):**
1. **Waist Width:** At the natural waistline
2. **Hip Width:** At the hips
3. **Thigh Width:** Around the thigh
4. **Knee Width:** Around the knee
5. **Leg Opening Width:** Around the ankle
6. **Front Rise:** From waist to crotch seam in front
7. **Back Rise:** From waist to crotch seam in back
8. **Inseam Length:** From crotch to hem inside the leg

## Conclusion

X Suit's sizing methodology represents a modern approach to online clothing fit, combining AI prediction with traditional measurement accuracy. While their AI system prioritizes convenience through minimal data input, the comprehensive size charts provide detailed measurement information for customers who prefer traditional sizing methods. The system is designed to serve a wide range of body types with extensive size options and multiple length classifications, supported by robust customer service for sizing assistance.

The methodology successfully addresses the key challenges of online suit fitting by offering both quick AI-based recommendations and detailed measurement-based selection, backed by a generous fit guarantee and alteration policies that encourage confident purchasing decisions.

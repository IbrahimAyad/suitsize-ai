- **Garment Properties**: Considers material stretchiness and fit characteristics

4. **Brand Learning**: Algorithm learns from retailer data over time

#### **Customer Data Requirements:**
- Height and weight (estimated)
- General body type/fit preferences
- Previous fit experiences
- Brand sizing familiarity
- Optional: Athletic build, body shape indicators

#### **Unique Features:**
- **No Physical Measurements**: Completely eliminates need for measuring tapes
- **Body Shape Intelligence**: Detects and adapts to unique body shapes

- **Material Awareness**: Adjusts recommendations based on fabric properties
- **A/B Testing Integration**: Built-in experimentation capabilities

- **Length Variations**: Short/Regular/Long options for all sizes
- **Inseam Options**: Multiple inseam lengths for pants (28"-34")

#### **Data Collection Process:**
1. **AI Quick Method**: Height, weight, age only (no measurements)
2. **Traditional Method**: Comprehensive body measurements
3. **Interactive Tools**: Visual guides with unit conversion
4. **Precision Measurement**: 0.1 inch accuracy for all measurements

#### **Customer Data Requirements:**
**AI Method (Minimal):**
- Height (5'6" to 6'4" optimized range)
- Weight
- Age

**Traditional Method (Comprehensive):**
- **Jacket Measurements (8 points):**

- **Pants Measurements (8 points):**
  - Waist, Hip, Thigh, Knee measurements
  - Inseam, Outseam, Ankle measurements
  - Rise measurements

#### **Size Range:**
- **Jacket Sizes**: 34-54 (XS to 6XL)
- **Pants Waist**: 28-46
- **Length Options**: Short/Regular/Long
